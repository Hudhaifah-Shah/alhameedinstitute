Terms and Conditions

After registering for online classes on Al-Hameed Islamic Institute, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

  • First class will be a free trial.
  • There will be no registration fee.
  • The fees will be in $ US Dollars.
  • Fees must be paid within 5 days of due date or else classes will be ceased.
  • Monthly fees will be paid in advance.
  • If the student drops the course before the first class, there will be 100% refund.
  • If a class is missed without prior notice, there will be no makeup class.
  • To cancel a class, you will have to notify the teacher 1 -2 hours before hand which will allow you to arrange a makeup class.
  • Never share you you personal information with any teacher on whatsapp, it will be asked through email (alhameedislamic@gmail.com).
